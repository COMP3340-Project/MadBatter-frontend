Sustainability


We at the Mad Batter care about the environment, that's why we are consistently pushing to make our processes more environmentally sustainable.

Our products are packed in recycled paper/cardboard packaging when available. When we must use plastic, it is biodegradable plastic, so it won't fill up land fills.

We source all our ingredients from local farms and suppliers, lowering the carbon footprint of our ingredients

Unused ingredients and extra product gets donated to local food shelters to ensure that nothing goes to waste.
