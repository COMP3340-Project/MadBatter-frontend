Our Return Policy

We do not accept return on any products, however if there is an issue, please follow the steps below to receive a refund.


Step 1:
Take a photo of your product and include it in an email

Step 2:
In your email include the reason you are requesting a refund

Step 3:
We will reach out to you with the status of your refund once your request has been processed. Depending on your claim, we will either provide a full refund, store credit, or no refund if the claim is not valid.
